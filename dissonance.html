About us

Emerging from Spain, the duo Dissonance kicked off their project with debut track "From Dust" in Sebjak's label Biblioth√®que on October 2017, setting the foundation for their melodic-driven progressive tech style. Full of powerful harmonies and fat basslines, their style is the natural evolution of their previous separate ventures.
